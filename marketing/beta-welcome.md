Welcome to the AgroYield Network Beta. You're one of the first ~50 people testing what we hope becomes the default professional network for Nigerian agribusiness.

I'm Chijioke, the founder. I'm sending this from my actual inbox, not a no-reply address — if anything is broken, confusing, or missing, hit reply and tell me. I read every message before lunch.

Here's what's new this week, and why each one matters:

**The Verified Badge** — when we approve your business, you get a checkmark on your public profile. Buyers trust verified suppliers. We verify CAC numbers and physical addresses manually for now; turnaround is under 48 hours.

**Business Reviews** — your customers can leave a review on your public page. We moderate spam and remove anything obviously fake, but otherwise reviews are real and public. Five 4-star reviews are worth more than a hundred banner ads.

**Recurring Invoices** — set up monthly retainers, weekly deliveries, or quarterly subscriptions and we'll auto-generate the invoice on schedule. Daily cron at 06:00 WAT sweeps for anything due. You can pause, resume, or end any series from the recurring invoices page.

**Scan a Receipt → Expense** — this is the one I'm most excited about. Take a photo of any receipt with your phone, hit "Scan Receipt" on the Expenses page, and Claude reads the vendor, amount, date, and category in a few seconds. You review and save. No more typing receipts at month-end.

A few honest notes about Beta:

- Free tier gets 20 receipt scans/month, Pro gets 100, Growth is unlimited
- Printed receipts work better than handwritten ones — confidence is around 85% on the former, 40% on the latter
- We assume Nigerian Naira; foreign currency receipts surface raw numbers with a warning
- If something looks wrong, the receipt photo is saved so you can compare what we extracted against the original

Where to start (10 minutes):

1. Sign in at https://agroyield.africa
2. Open or create your business profile, request the verified badge from the Business tab
3. Send one invoice (or set up a recurring one if you have a regular customer)
4. Snap a receipt from yesterday and try the OCR — you'll see what I mean

How to give feedback:

- Bugs / weird behaviour → "Report a problem" button at the bottom of any page (creates a support ticket I see immediately)
- Feature requests / general thoughts → just reply to this email
- Critical issues (can't sign in, payment broken) → text me on WhatsApp at {{founder_phone}} during WAT business hours

Thank you for being here. Beta users are the most important users we'll ever have, because what you tell us in the next few weeks shapes what AgroYield becomes for the next few years.
